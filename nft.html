CommodityRepoNFT

We are now on blockchain with a disruptive type of NFT: CommodityRepoNFT

What is a NFT? Is a Non-Fungible Token, a smart contract deployed in the Ethereum Blockchain, and also called a Digital Asset. This environment is known as Descentralized Finance (DeFi) and relies on the security of the blockchain technology to allow the direct transation between peers.

A NFT can be linked to all sort of Tangible or Untangible assets, such as Real Estate or Art works.

In our case, we are linking the NFT to a Hard Wood Trading Contract.

CommodityRepoNFT acts like a Repurchase Agreement (AKA Repo, in Financial Markets), which means that the issuer (General Goods) will repurchase the NFT after a certain period of time with a previously agreed yield.

Is a short-term with fixed return type of NFT, 60 days maturity with a Annual Percentage Yield (APY) of 3% in USDC.

A few words about USDC: is a digital currency that is fully backed by U.S. dollar assets, which means that for every USDC in the crypto market there is one U.S. dollar in segregated accounts in U.S.-regulated financial institutions.

USDC is designed to be a stable coin and its price stability contrasts sharply with the notorious price fluctuations of other cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.
